Collaborations

We work with editorial partners, research institutions, and media organisations on a selective basis. Use the form to introduce yourself and describe what you have in mind.

What We Offer

ETW Editorial produces independent geopolitical analysis for a global, English-speaking audience. We have the capability to support editorial partners in the following ways:

  • Co-published or licensed analysis for media organisations
  • Custom research briefs for think tanks, policy organisations, or corporate clients
  • Sponsored analysis — clearly labelled, subject to full editorial independence
  • Media appearances, interviews, and expert commentary
  • Speaking engagements on geopolitical topics

Who We Work With

We are selective about the partners we work with. We prioritise organisations that share our commitment to accuracy, independence, and analytical rigour. We do not work with political campaigns, partisan advocacy groups, or any organisation that would compromise ETW Editorial's independence.

Past and potential collaboration categories include: media publishers, universities and research institutions, international affairs organisations, and corporate geopolitical advisory.

Editorial Independence

All analysis published under the ETW Editorial name reflects our independent judgement, regardless of the commercial relationship involved. Sponsored content is clearly labelled. We do not accept payment in exchange for favourable analysis, editorial positions, or political alignment.

If your inquiry involves influencing our editorial content, we are not the right partner.

Send an Inquiry

We review all serious inquiries and respond to those that are a good fit. We do not publish contact email addresses to avoid spam.
