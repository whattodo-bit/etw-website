03 — Video Channel

YouTube

History is not the past. It is the instruction manual for the present.

The ETW YouTube channel is a long-form history project — not a news feed, not a personality. Each video is a single narrative built from primary sources, scripted with care, and made to reward attention.

Launching Soon
The channel is launching soon. The first video is in production.

The scope is deliberately wide: civilisations, wars, economies, empires, the decisions that shaped the century we inherited. The format is deliberately serious: no outrage, no hot takes, no filler.
